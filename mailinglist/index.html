The school only allows singups via our old rusty list serve. Here are instructions to help you out.

We send out important information about our events, workshops, talks and meetings via email on our mailing list. To stay in the loop, we highly recommend subscribing to the list.

Subscribing

Head over to our group's mailing list signup page. It should look something like this.

Once you're on that page, fill in the form with your email address and name. No one except ACM officers and administrators can access this information, so it is fairly safe. You do not necessarily have to pick a password. If you haven't, a password will be mailed to you. You might not have to cisit this page often, so just in case you forgot the password you used, the following section will come of help.

Subscriptions are purged every semester so do sign up again at the beginning of every semeseter to recieve our weekly emails.

Password Reminders, Unsubscribing and other Subscription Options

Head back to the group's mailing list signup page and look for the field in the bottom. Fill in your email and hit the button to select an option to get a password reminder, unsibscribe or change other options.
